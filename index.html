How to Find Us

We are located on Manhattan's west side on 42nd Street and 12th Avenue - walking distance from Times Square.

Public Transportation By Subway

A, C, E, 1, 2, 3, 7, S, N, Q, and R trains to Times Square 42nd Street. Walk or take the bus to 12th Avenue.

By Bus

Take the westbound M42 or M50 bus and get off at the last stop 42nd Street and 12th Avenue.

BY CAR OR MOTORCOACH

From George Washington Bridge, Westchester or Connecticut

Take the Henry Hudson Parkway (Route 9A), go south to West 43rd Street. Make a right at 43rd Street to Pier 83.

From Lincoln Tunnel, New Jersey

Upon exiting the tunnel, keep to the left and on the 3rd light turn left onto 42nd Street. Turn right on 10th Avenue and turn left on 43rd Street. Go west on 43rd Street across the highway to Pier 83.

From Midtown Tunnel, Queens and Long Island

Take 2nd Avenue downtown to 34th Street. Take a right on 34th Street to 10th Avenue. Go right on 10th Avenue and left on 43rd Street. Go straight on 43rd Street to Pier 83.
